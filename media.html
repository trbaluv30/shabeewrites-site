A Girlfriend Chat with Author Shabitha( ShaBee)

In this episode of Bookethon- S2, we welcome author Shabitha, also known as Shabee, a passionate storyteller who brings emotion and heartfelt moments into her writing. Her stories are beautiful. Her stories are characterised by a rebirth, healing, and personal development. Join us as we dive into Shabee's journey as a writer and her inspiration behind storytelling.
